Dragon Ball — Catbox Image Packer

Fetches every catbox image, verifies it, and builds a ZIP — all in one pass. Multi-image characters are transformation galleries (Base → forms); each image is labelled with its form and filed as Images/<character>/NN_<form>.jpg. Phone-safe: reads & shrinks each image the instant it arrives (nothing is held as a live blob), so one modest ZIP comes out at the end. When it finishes, tap the green save link.
