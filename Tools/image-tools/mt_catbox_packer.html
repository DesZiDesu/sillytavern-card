Mushoku Tensei — Catbox Image Packer

Downloads all 35 Mushoku Tensei portraits from catbox (which works in your browser, even though it is blocked from Claude's sandbox), verifies each one, and builds a ZIP. Characters with life-stage forms are filed as Images/<character>/NN_<form>.jpg. Paced with automatic retries. The green save link appears only when everything is finished — leave the tab open until then, then download the ZIP and send it back to Claude to store in the repo.
